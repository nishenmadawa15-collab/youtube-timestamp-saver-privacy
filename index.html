Privacy Policy

YouTube Timestamp Saver • Last Updated: November 2024

Privacy First

We do NOT collect any personal data. All your bookmarks are stored locally on your device.

Overview

YouTube Timestamp Saver is a browser extension that helps you save and organize timestamps from YouTube videos. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Data Collection

Our extension does NOT collect any data:

  • ✕ Does NOT collect personal information
  • ✕ Does NOT track your browsing activity
  • ✕ Does NOT send data to external servers
  • ✕ Does NOT use analytics or tracking tools
  • ✕ Does NOT require account registration
  • ✕ Does NOT display advertisements

Data Storage

All your bookmarks and notes are stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. This data:

  • ✓ Stays on your device only
  • ✓ Is never transmitted to us or any third party
  • ✓ Is completely under your control
  • ✓ Can be exported or deleted at any time

Permissions Explained

Our extension requires minimal permissions to function:

Permission Why We Need It
storage To save your bookmarks locally on your device
activeTab To read the current video timestamp when you click "Save"
scripting To interact with YouTube's video player
host_permissions To work only on YouTube website

Data Sharing

We do NOT share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data in the first place.

Your Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Export: Download all your bookmarks as a JSON file
  • Import: Restore bookmarks from a backup file
  • Delete: Clear all bookmarks with one click
  • Uninstall: Removing the extension deletes all local data

Children's Privacy

Our extension does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
